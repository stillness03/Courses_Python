Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve answered the most common questions from our future students

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment terms. You can pay in full with a discount or split the payment into several parts. We also partner with banks to provide education loans.

Is there a job guarantee?

We provide a job guarantee for students of the intensive format who successfully complete the course and all practical tasks. If you don’t find a job within 3 months after graduation, we refund 100% of the tuition fee.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, you can request a refund within 14 days after the course starts if the format doesn’t suit you or you cannot continue. After this period, refunds are only possible in exceptional cases.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, all students who successfully complete the course and pass the final exam receive a certificate. The certificate contains course details, acquired skills, and can be verified by employers via a QR code.
